Eric Orchard

Eric Orchard lives in Halifax, NS, with his wife, Julie. He studied painting and art history at the Nova Scotia College of Art and Design. Eric has illustrated two othee picture books. A Forest for Christmas by Michael Harris and Anything But Hank by Zachariah Wells and Rachel Lebowitz.
