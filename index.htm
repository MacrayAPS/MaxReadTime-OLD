Notes from the President - Cathie Hubley

The interest in books was instilled in me at an early age and I learned to love reading. Fond memories of my Father reading to us at bedtime still stay with me. He gave all eight of us a great advantage in school, as well as provided us with many wonderful hours of reading entertainment. Books allow people young and old to use their imaginations and cognitive skills in a rich, rewarding and informative environment.

If you have any suggestions or further questions about our products and services please feel free to contact us. We are always looking to improve.

WELCOME

At MAX ReadTime Ltd. we provide an interactive reading experience for all your loved ones.This unique concept was developed for families that for one reason or another are not able to read to their children because they are separated by distance. Grandparents missing the joy of reading to their grandchildren, parents that need to travel for business and want the bedtime story experience, and troops that may be away from their children during their most formative years. MAX ReadTime is a unique reading solution for people who value time with their children and want to provide them with an enriched reading environment.

"Reading is a fundamental skill that is necessary for growth, development and education and should be available to all ages everywhere."

These products are designed for ease of use for all ages. The large buttons, user friendly interface and simple instructions make it ideal to use for children and adults of any age. You can choose to do a reading session with a child using our live video option allowing you to interact with the child and enjoy the story telling and learning experience with them. An additional feature lets you record your reading session to send by email for the child to hear later, or as a surprise. Try our guided TOUR to learn more about MAX ReadTime and our reading products.
