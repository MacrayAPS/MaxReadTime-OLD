FAQ

Question:
My video is very dark. How can I fix it?

Answer:
Make sure there is adequate light in the room.

Question:
My video is not showing in the little window. Where is my video?

Answer:
Check to make sure your camera is connected. Ensure the camera is pointing towards you at an angle that will show your picture.

Question:
No videio is showing up for either myself or the person I am reading to.

Answer:
Make sure you have installed the newest version of Flash Player. Go to Adobe Flash Player Downlaod Center 

Question:
My video is very blurred.

Answer:
There may be a focus ring on the front of your camera. Turn it to adjust your focus. Also to much direct light behind the video subject may cause blurring. Make sure you or they are sitting in an area that has the light to the side, behind or above the camera.

Question:
There is a delay in the audio when I am reading the book.

Answer:
There may be a slight delay due to video streaming technology. Wait a few seconds before turning a page to let the person receiving the video catch up.
