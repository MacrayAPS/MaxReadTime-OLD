Featured Authors

Carrie Muller

The author of the newly released "the terrible, horrible, smelly Pirate", Carrie Muller grew up in Cape Breton and now lives in Dartmouth, NS., where she is employed as a library assistant. Carrie has been bringing her love of literature to children through stories, puppet shows, and general zaniness for many years.

Jacqueline Halsey

Co-Author of "the terrible, horrible, smally, Pirate," Jacqueline Halsey is a writer, artist, and avid reader. Her book Peggy's Letters was nominated for three children's choice book awards, Hackmatack, Rocky Mountain and Silver Birch. For the last fifteen years, she has worked as a programmer in the youth services department of Alderney Gate Library. She lives in Beaver Bank, Nova Scotia .
